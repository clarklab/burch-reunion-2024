Welcome

The 2024 Burch Family Reunion will take place July 19-23 at the Innsbrook Village Country Club & Resort in Ruidoso, New Mexico! We'd love for you to join us, and hope this page has all the information needed to make your trip an easy one.

This year, we've added a few extra days for those that can make it. The main festivities are Friday, Saturday, and Sunday, July 21-23, 2024. Some of us will also be there early, starting Wednesday, July 19. Whatever works, join us when you can!
